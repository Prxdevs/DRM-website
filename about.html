Our Story

Jashn- A Treasure Trove of Pure, Aromatic, and Premium Quality Rice

The seed of Jashn Foods was sown on a Sunday brunch when the founders were relishing the delightful combination of Rajma Chawal (Kidney beans and rice). During the meal, they struck up a conversation on how rice is so versatile that it complements many flavours worldwide. This conversation sparked an idea — to bring premium quality rice to all, transforming those delightful meals with families into cherished experiences. That’s how Jashn Foods was born, and the journey to bringing the exotic flavours of basmati rice — from the paddy fields to your plate, took off. At Jashn Foods, we combine technology, innovation, and the century-old cultivation process to bring you high-quality, flavourful, and aromatic rice. We focus on being gentle to the earth and the corresponding environment to instill a sense of satisfaction in you and ourselves.
