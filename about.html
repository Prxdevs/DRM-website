Our Story

Founded in 1970, DRM – Shree Dwarkesh rice mill has been a trusted name in the rice industry for over five decades. With a deep-rooted commitment to excellence, we specialize in delivering premium-quality rice that meets the highest standards of purity, taste, and nutrition. Our journey reflects a legacy of trust, consistency, and customer satisfaction. At DRM, we believe in combining traditional values with modern technology to ensure that every grain we produce adds nourishment and flavor to meals across the country.
